What is SEO?

The process of modifying attributes of a website to make it more accessible to Google is called Search Engine Optimization. It is a backend process that molds a website to make it adhere to the algorithms of Google making it easier to be found as and when browsers make a search for keywords related to your business.

SEO Benefits

Search Engine Optimization would help a website gain high rankings on Google. This would result in the site getting targeted quality traffic coming to it, thereby boosting the chances of generating business inquiries via the Internet.
